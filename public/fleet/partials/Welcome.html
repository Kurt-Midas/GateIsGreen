Security:

DO NOT USE THIS FOR ANYTHING IMPORTANT

  • If you share the fleet link (or someone guesses it) they'll have full access
  • You just gave a stranger (me) complete read and write access to your active fleet.

Remove this access by disbanding the fleet, changing the fleet boss, or using the Eve support site to remove the app's permissions
